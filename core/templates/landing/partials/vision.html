Why We Built HRTech

A story of solving the hardest problem in recruiting: finding the right people, reliably.

01

The Problem

Traditional recruiting relies on keyword matching. A candidate with "Python" in their CV gets an automatic match for "Python" roles. But what about the person who built distributed systems in Rust, architected APIs in Python, and now wants to lead a backend team? Keyword systems miss 35% of qualified candidates while simultaneously flooding recruiters with false positives.

02

The Insight

What if we could model skills and experience relationships like a knowledge graph? Instead of matching keywords, we could understand that someone who built real-time systems has the foundational knowledge for streaming infrastructure. That a DevOps engineer with container orchestration experience could architect cloud-native applications. This isn't just pattern matching—it's understanding the underlying principles.

03

Our Solution

HRTech combines AI-powered resume analysis with knowledge graphs to extract the true skill ecosystem of each candidate. We model skills as nodes connected by genuine relationships—not just keywords. The result: mathematical precision in candidate matching, combined with human insight. Recruiters find the right people 3x faster, and qualified candidates finally get discovered.
